Storage Tips

Pack Smart, Store Easy

A few pointers from our team to help you choose the right space and keep your belongings in great shape.

Picking the right size

  • 10×15 — a studio or small apartment's worth of furniture.
  • 10×20 — contents of a one-bedroom home.
  • 10×30+ — vehicles, business inventory, or a multi-room move.

Pack to protect

  • Use uniform box sizes and label every side.
  • Heaviest boxes on the bottom, fragile on top.
  • Leave a center aisle so you can reach the back.

Cold-weather storage

  • Drain hoses, fuel, and water lines on equipment.
  • Keep items off the floor on pallets.
  • For sensitive items, choose a climate-controlled unit.

Storing a vehicle or RV

  • Top off the tank and add fuel stabilizer.
  • Inflate tires and disconnect the battery.
  • Give it a wash — moisture and grime cause rust.

Make access easy

  • Store seasonal items near the door.
  • Keep an inventory list on your phone.
  • Visit during gate hours: 6 AM – 11 PM daily.

Keep it secure

  • Use a quality disc lock.
  • Never share your personal gate code.
  • Consider tenant insurance for valuables.
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you rent. Still have a question? Reach us at 509-304-9509.

What are your access hours?+

The gate is open for tenants every day from 6 AM to 11 PM. Our office is staffed Tuesday–Saturday, 9 AM to 5 PM.

Is there a long-term contract?+

No. All rentals are month-to-month, so you only pay for the time you need. Cancel any time with proper notice.

How do I pay my bill?+

Pay online through the Tenant Portal, or set up automatic payments so your rent is handled each month. Click "Pay Bill / Login" at the top to get started.

How does gate access work?+

You'll receive a personalized entry code at move-in. You can always view or reset your code from the Tenant Portal.

Can I store a vehicle, RV, or boat?+

Absolutely. We offer dedicated outdoor parking for RVs, trailers, boats, and equipment, plus larger drive-up units for vehicles. See the RV / Parking page for details.

Are the units secure?+

The facility is fully fenced with a code-operated gate and 24/7 video surveillance. We recommend a disc lock and tenant insurance for added peace of mind.

What's the difference between climate-controlled and drive-up?+

Climate-controlled units keep temperature and humidity stable — ideal for electronics, documents, and furniture. Drive-up units let you pull right up to the door, great for vehicles and bulky items.

How soon can I move in?+

Reserve a unit online and you can typically move in the same day during gate hours. You're all set the moment your reservation is confirmed.
